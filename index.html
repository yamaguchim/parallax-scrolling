MUDPIE

9:00 AM

Mudpie wakes up and hops onto a lilypad to catch some flies for breakfast.

12:00 PM

After breakfast, Mudpie watches dragonflies fly through the jungle. Then, he naps for a couple of hours.

5:00 PM

When the sun begins to set, Mudpie wakes up and ribbits along with the noises of the other jungle animals.

8:00 PM

Mudpie is exhausted from ribbiting along with his jungle friends. He finds a cozy leaf to sleep under before the scary animals emerge.
